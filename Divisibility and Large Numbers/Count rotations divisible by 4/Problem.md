# Count rotations divisible by 4

Count rotations divisible by 4

Examples:

 > Input: 8
 > Output: 1
 >
 > Input: 20
 > Output: 1
 > Rotation: 20 is divisible by 4
 >            02 is not divisible by 4
 >
 >  Input : 13502
 >  Output : 0
 >  No rotation is divisible by 4
 >
 >  Input : 43292816
 >  Output : 5
 >  5 rotations are : 43292816, 16432928, 81643292
                    92816432, 32928164
